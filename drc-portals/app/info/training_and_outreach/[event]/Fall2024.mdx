# CFDE 2024 Fall Meeting
Bethesda, Maryland<br/>
October 22 - 23, 2024

![image](https://cfde-drc.s3.amazonaws.com/assets/img/cfde-fall-2024.png)

This meeting provides an opportunity to get together and discuss strategic issues for Common Fund programs as well as goals for the upcoming year. 
The agenda will feature announcements about upcoming data from Bridge2AI, SenNet, HubMAP, and MoTrPAC, along with multiple brainstorming sessions discussing the future of CFDE.

Additionally, special topics will include building methods for sustainability and harmonizing CFDE data to answer integrative questions.
