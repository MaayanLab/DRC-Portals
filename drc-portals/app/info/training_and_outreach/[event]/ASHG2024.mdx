# CFDE at ASHG 2024 Annual Meeting
Denver, Colorado<br/>
November 5-9, 2024

![image](https://cfde-drc.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/assets/img/ASHG-2024.png)

The CFDE was showcased at the American Society of Human Genetics Annual (ASHG) Meeting by members of the Knowledge Center (KC), the Data Resource Center (DRC), Kids First DCC, MoTrPAC DCC, and other CFDE representatives. 


### Booths
KC, DRC, and other CFDE DCCs were showcased at booth **#571** and Kids First DCC at **#1120**.
![image](https://cfde-drc.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/assets/img/ashg-booth-2024.jpg)

## **Ancillary Session**
11:45am - 1:15pm (MST), November 06, 2024

An ancillary session at ASHG was held to share the perspectives about the the NIH Common Fund Data Ecosystem (CFDE) by NIH representatives, the Knowledge Center (KC), the Data Resource Center (DRC), and the MoTrPAC data coordination center (DCC).
